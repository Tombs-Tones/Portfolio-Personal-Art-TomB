Every piece in this collection is a moment of exploration • 5 to 30 minutes of imperfect creation. I draw to stay grounded, to study the human form, and to celebrate the beauty of making a mess. Drawn/painted works are entirely by hand: most on paper, some created digitally through Adobe Fresco, and others brought to life as public murals. Alongside these drawings, you’ll also find photography by me • usually arrangements that inspire my sketches. This portfolio is my visual journal: a space where I can be a little chaotic and explore.
