About the Artist

I'm Thomas Berg, and my work is an exploration of the human form, often captured through intense, dynamic line work. My creative process is fueled by a desire for connection, translating raw energy into simple, powerful representations.

Whether it's a quick, intimate "doodlers doodling" sketch or a large-scale project, the focus remains on capturing motion and emotion. Art is fundamentally a dialogue, and I aim to create pieces that speak to the restlessness and beauty of being human.
